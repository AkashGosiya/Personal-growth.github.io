Journaling is the simple yet sometimes tricky method (if you're going through or have gone through something horrible) to get back to your normal mental health or improve your mind structure. Definition of journaling changes according to the type of journaling you use. But for instance, it's potting your thoughts into paper and reflecting on yourself. There are many benefits of journaling and sometimes your situation and your willingness to change yourself determine your progress in getting benefits. Let's assume that you're going through some stress because of your workload at your workplace. So journaling will simply give you reflections of thoughts and you will be able to arrange and it will help you make better decisions for your good. How to Journal

  1. Decide your place:You can start journaling wherever and whichever place you want But having a certain place to write every day is a good idea it will help you be consistent.
  2. Getting your Journal:There are many options available, You can buy online or offline whichever you like. Choose the right Journal according to your interest It connects you with your journal. But you can always start with any blank page.
  3. Start with Small: Small start means giving At least 5 minutes a day(if you are a busy person) but If you are ready to give it a good shot then don't stop until you satisfy. And consistency is more important than Quantity.
  4. Reflect:After writing the whole thing you can see and get insights to make your day more positive.
  5. Patience:What is left is having patience and belief in the hard work you put into making your journal.

Types of Journaling

Let's look into the most desired Journal types to Improve your mental health.

Gratitude Journal:

It is a very refreshing and simple way to conquer everyday problems. I believe there is always something good in everything you just have to see it appropriately. Starting Gratitude Journal good idea for those who want to start their day with positivity. Have you ever wondered how many things you are grateful for? It could be things that you might come across daily. Every day you might come across bad news and a gratitude journal will pull you out of the negative tunnel and refresh you by being grateful for the things you have in your hand. It's simple to do this journal just sit with pen and paper see your surrounding or try to remember your past day and observe what are the things you are grateful for. It could be Huge support you get from colleagues, or It could be a Tiny but precise thing like getting smile from children. Size, Quantity, or Look is not important when feeling grateful. If you start Journaling in the morning then you can expect a positive start and If you start Journaling before going to sleep it will help you sleep better with making quality sleep.

Bullet Journal:

Having an unorganized way of working will probably give you stress. To tackle your work and mental health bullet journaling could be your destination. Start with Getting your book and pen ready. Your Bullet Journal can be a good tracker, a to-do list, a calendar, or a diary. Consider before starting your journal with the one best serve you. For example, you can write down activities that get you moving, and plan healthy meals. and keep tracking your weekly, and yearly step goals. Bullet Journal is tracking and implying effective steps toward your goal. Having organized work also means having a healthy brain.

Vision Journal:

Basically, It's a journal you create in order to clarify your vision for your Life, brand, passion projects, and Business. It helps you organize and keep track of your goals to break them down into daily steps. Do you know the most caused thing of bad mental health is? It's Guilt. The guilt of not taking action towards your dream life. Vision Journal is the one you can do for a long time to consistently take steps toward your dream. People say journaling holds magic, Once you write something down, It says on the page as well as in your mind. it keeps you on track by making steps to your path. You might hear this saying that Never Looks Back on life, But it's the opposite here you can look at your accomplishments of last month or year. It will fuel you to take a step further.

Personal Junk Journal:

This Journal is the opposite of a gratitude journal but still shares significant benefits. Gratitude Journal is for cheering your happy moments. When Personal Junk Journal is for venting your frustrations, stressful events, bad moods, and negative energy. It will help you take out your tensions or negative sentiments from inside when you are going through a hard time. It's an excellent way to vent your stress and negative energy and the best weapon to cop challenging times for a person who is going through a rough time or experiencing stress.
